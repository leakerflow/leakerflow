## Article Creation Workflow

### Phase 1: Information Gathering (30 minutes)

**Source Monitoring:**
```
✅ Official Rockstar channels
✅ Verified industry insiders (Tom Henderson, Jason Schreier)
✅ Gaming forums (GTAForums, Reddit r/GTA6)
✅ Social media trending topics
✅ Patent filings and job listings
```

**Credibility Assessment Matrix:**
- **🟢 CONFIRMED**: Official statements, verified footage
- **🟡 PROBABLE**: Reliable insider sources, corroborated rumors
- **🟠 RUMOR**: Single source, unverified claims
- **🔴 SPECULATION**: Fan theories, wishful thinking

### Phase 2: Content Planning (15 minutes)

**Article Structure Template:**
1. **Hook** (50 words): Compelling opening statement
2. **Executive Summary** (100 words): Key points overview
3. **Main Content** (800-1,200 words): Detailed analysis
4. **Community Impact** (200 words): Fan reactions and implications
5. **Conclusion** (100 words): Takeaways and future outlook

### Phase 3: Writing & Optimization (45 minutes)

**SEO Strategy:**
- **Primary Keywords**: "GTA 6 [specific topic]", "Grand Theft Auto 6 [feature]"
- **Long-tail Keywords**: "When is GTA 6 coming out", "GTA 6 map size comparison"
- **Trending Terms**: Monitor Google Trends for emerging search patterns

**Content Guidelines:**
- **Tone**: Authoritative yet accessible
- **Voice**: Active, engaging, conversational
- **Credibility Markers**: Clear source attribution throughout
- **Visual Integration**: Screenshots, concept art, comparison charts

## Sample Article Creation: "GTA 6 Heist System Leak Analysis"

### Step 1: Information Assessment

**Source**: Anonymous developer leak via 4chan
**Credibility Level**: 🟠 **RUMOR**
**Verification Status**: Unconfirmed, awaiting corroboration
**Content**: Claims about enhanced heist planning mechanics

### Step 2: Article Framework

**Title Options:**
1. "GTA 6 Heist System: New Leak Reveals Revolutionary Planning Mechanics"
2. "Breaking: GTA 6 May Feature Most Advanced Heist System Yet"
3. "Exclusive: Inside GTA 6's Rumored Multi-Stage Heist Planning"

**Selected Title**: Option 1 (balanced credibility and engagement)

### Step 3: Content Development

**Opening Hook:**
"A new leak suggests GTA 6's heist system will revolutionize criminal gameplay with unprecedented planning depth and execution variety."

**Credibility Disclaimer:**
"⚠️ **RUMOR STATUS**: This information comes from an unverified source and should be treated as speculation until officially confirmed."

**Main Content Structure:**
- Leak details breakdown
- Comparison with GTA V heist system
- Technical feasibility analysis
- Community reaction compilation
- Potential impact on gameplay

### Step 4: Community Engagement Integration

**Interactive Elements:**
- **Poll**: "Which heist feature excites you most?"
- **Comment Prompt**: "What heist locations would you like to see?"
- **Social Sharing**: Pre-written tweets with key quotes

## Content Quality Assurance

### Pre-Publication Checklist

**Accuracy Verification:**
- [ ] All sources properly attributed
- [ ] Credibility levels clearly marked
- [ ] No unsubstantiated claims presented as fact
- [ ] Update timestamps included

**SEO Optimization:**
- [ ] Target keywords naturally integrated
- [ ] Meta description optimized (150-160 characters)
- [ ] Image alt text includes relevant keywords
- [ ] Internal linking to related articles

**Engagement Optimization:**
- [ ] Clear call-to-action included
- [ ] Social sharing buttons prominent
- [ ] Comment section enabled and moderated
- [ ] Newsletter signup integrated

### Post-Publication Protocol

**Immediate Actions (0-2 hours):**
- Monitor initial engagement metrics
- Respond to early comments
- Share across social media channels
- Track trending keywords

**Short-term Follow-up (2-24 hours):**
- Analyze traffic sources
- Engage with community discussions
- Update article if new information emerges
- Plan follow-up content based on engagement

**Long-term Monitoring (1-7 days):**
- Track search ranking improvements
- Measure conversion rates
- Identify successful content elements
- Plan content calendar updates
